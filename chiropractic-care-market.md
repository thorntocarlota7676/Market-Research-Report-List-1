<p><p>The chiropractic care market can be categorized into three main types: Maintenance Care, Corrective Care, and Relief Care. Maintenance Care focuses on sustaining optimal health and preventing future issues through regular adjustments. Corrective Care aims to address and heal specific spinal misalignments or injuries over a longer treatment period. Relief Care primarily seeks to alleviate immediate pain or discomfort, often through short-term treatments. Each type serves distinct patient needs, contributing to the overall wellness and health management strategies.</p></p>
