<p><p>The miniature solenoid valve market is segmented into various types based on their functionality. The 2-way solenoid valve, the most common type, facilitates the on/off flow control of fluids through a single inlet and outlet. The 3-way solenoid valve allows for directing fluid flow between two outlets, making it versatile for mixing or diverting applications. The 4-way solenoid valve has two inlets and two outlets, often used in actuators or for controlling air in pneumatic systems. Other types may include specialized valves designed for niche applications.</p></p>
