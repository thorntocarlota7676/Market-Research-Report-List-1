<p><strong>The Alarm Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Buzzer</li><li>Passive Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alarm buzzer market is primarily divided into active and passive buzzers. Active buzzers generate sound when powered, incorporating an internal oscillating circuit. They are commonly used in alarms and notifications due to their consistent output. In contrast, passive buzzers require an external oscillating source to produce sound, making them more versatile for varying tones and pitches. Both types find applications in security systems, consumer electronics, and home automation, catering to diverse sound signaling needs across various industries.</p></p>
